Tally Prime 5.0

Professional Accounting Course
Duration: 2 Months (Approx.)

Overview

Tally Prime 5.0 is one of the most widely used accounting software in India. This course helps students learn complete accounting concepts and practical usage of Tally Prime for business, office, and professional accounting jobs.

Objective

Upon completion of this course, the participant will be able to manage company accounts, generate reports, handle GST billing, payroll, inventory, and perform real-time accounting operations using Tally Prime 5.0.

Target Audience

Students / Commerce Students / Office Staff / Accountants / Business Owners / Job Seekers

Prerequisites

No prior accounting software knowledge is required.
