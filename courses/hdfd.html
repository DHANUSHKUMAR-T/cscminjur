HDFD

Honours Diploma in Full Stack Developer
Duration: 1 Year

Overview

This course is designed for beginners to specialize in problem solving skills with programming languages and building complete modern websites with Front-End, Back-End and Web Framework technologies along with Database Management Systems.

Upon completion of this course, you are 100% fit for campus placements.

Objective

After completing the program, you will be eligible for a variety of career opportunities and build your career in Front-End Developer, Back-End Developer, API, or Full Stack Developer roles in organizations ranging from start-ups to Fortune 500 companies.

Prerequisites

No prior computer knowledge is required.

Target Audience

School Final / Diploma / Graduates / Engineering Students
